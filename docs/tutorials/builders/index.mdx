# For AI builders

The Nevermined payments app allows to AI builders to receive payment for their AI agents. In this group of tutorials we will show how register your AI in the application and get paid.

If you are an AI builder and you plan to publish AI services in the app, you need to complete some simple steps:

1. [Configure your account](configure-account)
2. [Create a Subscription](create-subscription)
3. [Register your AI service](register-webservice)
4. and get paid!
