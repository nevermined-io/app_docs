# For AI Builders

The Nevermined payments app gives AI builders the ability to receive payment for their AI agents. In this group of tutorials, we will show how to register your AI using the Nevermined app in order to get paid.

If you are an AI Builder and you plan to get paid for your AI services using the app, you need to complete some simple steps:

1. [Configure your account](configure-account)
2. [Create a Subscription](create-subscription)
3. [Register your AI service](register-webservice)
4. and get paid!
